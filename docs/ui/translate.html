翻译功能

中文 English
标准翻译 书面语

"I want to go to a coffee shop to buy a latte, but I don't know how to order in English."

地道口语 推荐

"Hey, I'd like to grab a latte from the coffee shop, but I'm not sure how to order it in English."

更自然的表达:

• "grab a latte" 比 "buy a latte" 更口语化
• "I'm not sure" 比 "I don't know" 更礼貌

相关实用短语

Can I have a latte, please?

请给我一杯拿铁，好吗？

I'd like to order a coffee.

我想点一杯咖啡。

What would you recommend?

你推荐什么？

💡 翻译技巧

记住：口语翻译要自然流畅，不要逐字翻译。多用日常表达，让对话更地道！
